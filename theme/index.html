About KJSCE

Symphony, ‘the emotion of KJSCE’, is back with it’s virtual version known as the ‘Symphony Land’. The Land manifests itself only for a specific time of the year during which it spreads happiness far and wide. Comprising various islets, each of which hosts a jamboree of its own, the Symphony Land is also called the land of fete, fest and frolics! This fable of the Symphony Land is coming to life at Symphony 2021, ‘An Empyrean Saga’ wherein the heavenly land of Symphony will resurrect into a paradise on Earth for us all.

Developed with by Devansh Shah, Marmik Shah, Keval Sanghvi and Darshil Mehta
