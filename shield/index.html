Shield Dance

Description

The art of dance does not discriminate between anything, as it is the feeling that can be expressed without even saying a word. Are you ready to bring this enthralling art form to life while you compete for your branch?

Rules

General Rules:

  1. Event Category: One team per branch.
  2. Participation compulsory from each year: 2 FYs, 2 SYs, 2 TYs are compulsory.
    • Team size:6-12members.
    • Time limit:3-7min+(1min of solo dance).
    • A face-off which will consist of Solo performance of all branches will be following all group performances.
    • Teams may use more than one song in their performance.
    • Minimum of 1 and Maximum of 4 members could be in a frame , but social distancing should be maintained at all times.All the members in a frame should not touch each other.
    • Girls and boys should not touch each other throughout the dance.
    • Lifts, touching or sensuality is not permitted.
    • Minimum of 1 participants should be present throughout the video.
    • Obscenity/Vulgarity of any kind is not allowed and will lead to immediate disqualification. Songs should be chosen accordingly.
    • Decency in dressing should be maintained at all times. The minimum knee-length costume is allowed but with compulsory stockings. No shears/tears are allowed.
    • Usage of props is allowed(Subject to approval).
    • Any kind of fluid, flame or heavy object is not allowed during performance.

Music

Description

Come, serenade your fellow branches to loss as you take your band to the top and triumph over this musical land, a part of the grand Symphony Land!

Rules

General Rules:

  1. Last date for video submission: 16thMarch,2021
  2. Event category : One team per branch
  3. No of participants: 3-7.
  4. Compulsory participation: 1 from FY, SY, and TY each.

  9. Instrumental for 1 min is necessary.

  14. The participant should be visible in the camera frame throughout the video.
  15. Each branch will submit a recorded video of their performance.The team members should be visible in the camera frame throughout the video.Each team member will record his/her part individually.You may edit the video to have multiple team members on the screen.
  16. Video editing like adding special effects,texts in between is not allowed.
  17. Submission of previous performances will not be accepted.

Shield Art

Description

To all those aspiring artists out there, bored of painting alone in your room this entire lockdown? Want to get out & paint the world ? Shield Art is the platform for you, come out & paint your branch with the colour of victory!

Rules

General Rules:

  1. Event category: Multiple participants per branch.
  2. Compulsory participation: 1 drawing per branch.
  3. Participants will have to execute the artwork live.
  4. All participants must have a stable internet connection.
  5. Decency in dressing should be maintained at all times.
  6. Ensure that the frame is angled such that artwork and the body movements are clearly visible.
  7. Time limit-1 hour.
  8. Theme:An Empyrean Saga.The theme is open to interpretation.
  9. Mediums Required: Watercolours, poster colours, acrylic paint, black marker, pencil, charcoal pencils, non-water soluble colour pencils, A3 size paper
  10. Participants to use their own materials

Shield Photography

Description

Tired of seeing your camera sit in the corner of your room, covered in dust? Well, it’s time to grab it & set your lens to the right aperture because it’s the Shield brach wars and your branch awaits your photos to make them reach the hall of fame!

Rules

General Rules:

  1. Event category: Multiple participants per branch.
  2. Compulsory participation: 1 photo per branch per year.
  3. Phone Cameras are allowed
  4. Topic : Repetition of Objects
  5. No timestamps,signature allowed
  6. Basic editing is allowed. (Only Contrast, Brightness, and Saturation can be adjusted during post effects) Photo-morphing, manipulations or local editing is not allowed.
  7. Each photo should contain a caption of maximum 200 characters. Photos with obscene/vulgar captions shall be disqualified.
  8. 1 person can submit a maximum of up to 2 photographs.
  9. Entries should be submitted before the date 16th March,2021. Submission link will be shared shortly.

Shield Creative writing

Description

Penned down an entire book in this lockdown? Or wrote 100s of blogs? It’s time to get this expertise you’ve gained at writing into play & help your branch reach the top while you ink the stories of your mind palace!

Rules

General Rules:

  1. Theme: An Early Morning Walk in Winter
  2. Minimum 1 entry per branch.
  3. Maximum 3 entries per branch
  4. Entries should be submitted using the link before the date 16th March,2021.
  5. Every participant is allowed to submit only one entry.
  6. The entry must strictly be in English.
  7. The entry should be in only one verse: prose.
  8. No form of plagiarism will be entertained. If found, the entry will be discarded
  9. Microtales, poetry, and any other form of written verse will not be accepted.
  10. Each entry should consist of a minimum of 500 and a maximum of 2000 words.
  11. Each entry shall be judged on the following criteria:
    • Grammar and spelling
    • Theme incorporation
    • Originality of idea
    • Fluidity of prose
