Verve

Description

Dance is an eternal celebration of yourself, and it allows you to get in touch with your soul. Witness the startling performances of the dancers performing inexplicable stunts and moving their bodies in unison. Loosen your feet, and unleash the dancer within you at Verve, the group dance competition of Symphony!

Rules

General Rules

  1. Crews are allowed to participate, provided all participating members are college students, albeit not necessarily from the same college.
  2. Participants may use more than one song in their performance.
  3. Obscenity/Vulgarity of any kind is not allowed and will lead to immediate disqualification. Songs should be chosen accordingly.
  4. Decency in dressing should be maintained at all times. Minimum knee length costume is allowed but with compulsory stockings. No shears/tears are allowed.
  5. Usage of props is allowed. These have to be brought by the teams themselves but the organizers hold the discretion of allowing it on stage.
  6. Any kind of fluid, flame or heavy object is not allowed on stage.
  7. Teams are STRICTLY prohibited from mentioning their college names during the performance by any medium.
  8. Pre-recorded music should be emailed to kjscesymphony@somaiya.edu with the subject: “Verve- Team Name” latest by 27th January, 2019
  9. The email should have the following:
    1. The music in mp3 format.
    2. Team Name
    3. Participants’ Name
    4. Phone Numbers
    5. College name(s)
    6. Prop List (if any)
  10. Genre: Open
  11. Last date for registration: 27th January, 2019.
Crews are allowed to participate, provided all participating members are college students, albeit not necessarily from the same college.

Elimination Rules

  1. The dance to be performed in the elimination round should be a part of the final performance.
  2. Time limit:4-7 mins (from music on till turning the music off).
  3. Violation of any of the above general rules, may lead to immediate disqualification.

Final Rules

  1. Censor round will be held on the day of the event prior to the event.
  2. Teams must carry at least one backup of their pre-recorded music in a pen drive in mp3 format only.
  3. Time Limit: 12 mins (from music on till turning the music off).
  4. Time limit must strictly be observed. Failure to keep within the time limit will result in a penalty.
  5. For both Eliminations and Finals, 20% marks will be deducted if the performance exceeds by 15 secs. If the performance time increases further by 30 sec, it will lead to disqualification.
  6. The decision of the judges and organizers shall be final and binding.

Judging Criteria

  1. Choreography
  2. Energy
  3. Expression
  4. Synchronization
  5. Stage utilization
  6. Costumes
  7. Innovation
  8. Overall impact
